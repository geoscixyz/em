Properties
==========

The diagnostic physical properties for this case history are electrical conductivity and magnetic susceptibility. As we can see, the host rock is resistive and highly permeable. In contrast, the target (graphitic breccia pipes) are conductive and non-susceptible.

+-------------------------------+--------------------+---------------------+
| Rock Classification           | Conductivity (S/m) | Susceptibility (SI) |
+===============================+====================+=====================+
| Albany Alkalic Complex (Host) |         Low        |        High         |
+-------------------------------+--------------------+---------------------+
| Graphitic Breccia Pipes       |         High       |        Low          |
+-------------------------------+--------------------+---------------------+
| Unmineralized Sill            |         Low        |        Low          |
+-------------------------------+--------------------+---------------------+
